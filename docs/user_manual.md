## Getting Started

### Accessing the Application

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge recommended)
2. Navigate to the UWDialed application URL (typically `http://localhost:3000` for local development)
3. You'll see the UWDialed home page with the University of Waterloo logo

### First-Time Setup

If this is your first time using UWDialed, we recommend taking the survey to get personalized study spot recommendations. However, you can explore all study spots without taking the survey.

## Home Page

The home page is your starting point. From here, you can:

- **View Dashboard** - Browse all available study spots with detailed information
- **View Map** - See study spots on an interactive campus map
- **Take Survey** - Complete a quick survey to get personalized recommendations

Simply click on any of these options to navigate to the respective page.

## Taking the Survey

The survey helps UWDialed understand your study preferences and recommend the best spots for you.

### How to Take the Survey

1. Click **"Take Survey"** from the home page
2. You'll see a series of questions about your study preferences:
   - **Location Type** - Library, Caf√©, University building, etc.
   - **Travel Distance** - How far you're willing to travel
   - **Busyness Preference** - How busy you like your study environment
   - **Power Access** - Importance of power outlets
   - **Food Importance** - How important nearby food/drink options are
   - **Food Preference** - Type of food options you prefer
   - **Noise Level** - What noise level helps you focus
   - **Lighting** - Natural lighting preferences

3. **Answer each question** by clicking on your preferred option
4. The survey automatically advances to the next question after you select an answer
5. Use the **"‚Üê Previous"** button if you want to go back and change an answer
6. After completing all questions, your preferences are saved and recommendations are generated

### Survey Features

- **Progress Indicator** - Shows which question you're on (e.g., "Q 3/8")
- **Auto-Save** - Your preferences are automatically saved in your browser
- **Retake Survey** - If you've taken the survey before, you can view your saved preferences and choose to retake it

### After Completing the Survey

Once you finish the survey:
- Your preferences are saved for 30 days
- Personalized recommendations are generated
- You'll see a confirmation screen with a link to view your personalized dashboard

## Dashboard

The Dashboard is the main hub for exploring study spots. It displays all available study spots with detailed information and filtering options.

### Dashboard Features

#### Recommended Study Spots Section

If you've completed the survey, you'll see a **"Recommended for You"** section at the top showing study spots that match your preferences. These are prioritized based on your survey responses.

#### All Study Spots Section

Below the recommendations, you'll see all available study spots with:
- **Location Name** - The name of the study spot
- **Key Information** - Busyness level, noise level, power options, food options, and lighting
- **Quick View** - Click on any study spot card to see more details

#### Filtering Study Spots

Use the filter panel on the left to narrow down your search:

- **Busyness** - Filter by busyness level (Very Quiet, Moderately Busy, Busy/Active)
- **Noise Level** - Filter by noise level (Silent, Low Background, Moderate, Lively)
- **Power Options** - Filter by power outlet availability
- **Food Options** - Filter by nearby food/drink options
- **Lighting** - Filter by natural lighting availability

**To apply filters:**
1. Click on any filter dropdown
2. Select your desired option
3. The study spots list updates automatically
4. Click **"Reset Filters"** to clear all filters

#### Sorting Study Spots

Use the sort dropdown to organize study spots by:
- **Name** (A-Z)
- **Busyness** (Least to Most Busy)
- **Noise Level** (Quietest to Loudest)

#### Study Spot Details

Click on any study spot card to view detailed information:

- **Full Location Details** - Complete information about the spot
- **Interactive Map** - A mini-map showing the exact location
- **Reviews** - See what other students have said about this spot
- **Write a Review** - Share your own experience

**To close the detail view:** Click the "X" button or click outside the detail panel.

## Interactive Map

The Interactive Map page shows all study spots on a visual map of the University of Waterloo campus.

### Using the Map

1. **Navigate to the Map** - Click "View Map" from the home page
2. **View Study Spots** - All study spots are marked with pins on the map
3. **Zoom and Pan** - Use your mouse wheel to zoom, click and drag to pan around
4. **Click on Pins** - Click any pin to see the study spot name and basic information
5. **Hover for Details** - Hover over a pin to see a quick preview

### Map Features

- **Campus Overview** - See the entire University of Waterloo campus
- **Location Pins** - Each study spot is marked with a pin
- **Interactive Navigation** - Zoom in to see specific buildings and areas
- **Quick Information** - Get location details without leaving the map

## Writing Reviews

Help other students by sharing your experience at study spots!

### How to Write a Review

1. **Navigate to a Study Spot** - Go to the Dashboard and click on any study spot
2. **Scroll to Reviews Section** - In the detail view, find the "Reviews" section
3. **Fill Out the Review Form:**
   - **Your Name** - Enter your name (required)
   - **Rating** - Click on the stars to rate (1-5 stars)
   - **Review Text** - Write your review describing your experience (required)
4. **Submit** - Click the "Submit Review" button

### Review Guidelines

- Be honest and helpful
- Include specific details (e.g., "Great natural lighting in the morning")
- Mention any issues (e.g., "Can get noisy during lunch hours")
- Keep reviews respectful and appropriate

### Viewing Reviews

- Reviews are displayed in the study spot detail view
- Reviews show the reviewer's name, star rating, and review text
- Reviews are sorted by most recent first

## Tips and Best Practices

### Getting the Best Recommendations

1. **Complete the Survey** - The more accurate your survey responses, the better your recommendations
2. **Update Your Preferences** - If your study habits change, retake the survey
3. **Use Filters** - Combine filters to find exactly what you're looking for

### Finding Study Spots

1. **Check Busyness Levels** - Avoid spots marked as "Busy/Active" during peak hours if you prefer quiet
2. **Consider Power Needs** - Filter by power options if you need to charge devices
3. **Read Reviews** - Check recent reviews to get current information about a spot
4. **Use the Map** - The map helps you find spots near your current location

### Contributing to the Community

1. **Write Reviews** - Share your experiences to help others
2. **Be Specific** - Detailed reviews are more helpful than generic ones
3. **Update Information** - If you notice something has changed, mention it in your review

### Troubleshooting

**Study spots not loading?**
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Ensure the backend server is running

**Map not displaying?**
- Check that you have a stable internet connection (Mapbox requires internet)
- Try refreshing the page

**Survey not saving?**
- Make sure cookies are enabled in your browser
- Try completing the survey again

**Can't see recommendations?**
- Make sure you've completed the survey
- Check that all survey questions were answered

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**  
A: No, UWDialed works without requiring an account. Your preferences are saved in your browser.

**Q: Can I use UWDialed on my phone?**  
A: Yes! UWDialed is responsive and works on mobile devices, though the full experience is optimized for desktop browsers.

**Q: How often should I retake the survey?**  
A: Retake the survey whenever your study preferences change, or if you want to explore different types of study spots.

**Q: Are the busyness levels updated in real-time?**  
A: Busyness levels are estimates based on historical data. For the most current information, check recent reviews.

**Q: Can I save favorite study spots?**  
A: Currently, UWDialed doesn't have a favorites feature, but you can bookmark study spots in your browser or take note of locations you like.
